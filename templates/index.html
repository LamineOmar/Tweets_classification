Dataset

Employing Arabic Darija datasets, our project focused on training a specialized sentiment analysis model catering to the linguistic nuances and cultural context of Arabic-speaking regions, enhancing the accuracy and relevance of sentiment analysis within these communities.

ML model

Within this project, we harnessed the power of machine learning algorithms to delve into the sentiments expressed within tweets. By leveraging these algorithms, we aimed to dissect and understand the emotional undercurrents inherent in each tweet, thereby unlocking a deeper understanding of the sentiments pervading social media discourse.

Deploiment

Following the completion of model training, we seamlessly integrated a simplified user interface for deployment, facilitating effortless accessibility and usability for a wide range of users. This intuitive interface was meticulously designed to streamline the interaction process, allowing users to harness the power of our sentiment analysis model with ease and efficiency.
